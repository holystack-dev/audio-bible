Audio Bible Installer

Install firmware on your round ESP32-S3 player — right from this page, nothing to download.

Your browser can't talk to USB devices. Please use Chrome or Edge on a computer (Windows, Mac, Linux, or Chromebook). iPhones and iPads can't flash USB devices.
  1. Plug the device into this computer with a USB-C cable.
  2. Click Connect & Install above.
  3. Pick the device's serial port when asked, then choose Install.
  4. Wait until it finishes, then unplug. That's it.

Can't connect? Put the board in download mode: hold BOOT, tap RESET, release BOOT, then try again.
Source & build-your-own guide: GitHub repo.
